Explore India's Literary Heart ?

Step into Kozhikode, a city where every corner holds a story and every experience is an invitation to discovery. Honored as India’s very first UNESCO City of Literature, our city is a vibrant canvas where the past and present come together — from ancient trade routes to modern literary voices. Let Kozhikode whisper its tales to you.

We Are on a Mission...

Unlocking Kozhikode's True Cultural Brilliance!

We are on a mission to bring Kozhikode's incredible story to the forefront. Did you know our beloved city is India's first UNESCO City of Literature?

It's a truly special title, yet one that hasn't received the spotlight it deserves. That's why we're embarking on an exciting journey to make Kozhikode the nation's premier Cultural Planned City!

Join us in celebrating our past, shaping a brilliant future, and putting Kozhikode firmly on the global map!

Where the World first landed!

This city is also where the world first landed centuries ago, putting us on the map! Now, we're on a mission to become India's first Planned Cultural City, and in doing so, help Kozhikode rise again to global fame. Join us – your support makes all the difference!
